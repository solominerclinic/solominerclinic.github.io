What We Fix

Repair Services

From simple diagnostics to full ASIC chip replacement. If it mines Bitcoin, we can fix it.

Diagnostics & Simple Repairs

Thermal paste reapplication, heatsink reseating, pin alignment, firmware reflash, fan cleaning, and general troubleshooting.

ASIC Chip Replacement

BM1366 (Ultra), BM1368 (Supra), BM1370 (Gamma, NerdQaxe, NerdOctaxe, NerdEko) chip replacement or reflow.

VRM Replacement

Voltage regulator replacement and power delivery board-level repair for voltage regulation failures.

Fan & Heatsink Upgrades

Active fan upgrades, custom heatsink installations, and thermal management improvements for better cooling.

Fuse & Component Replacement

Blown SMD fuse replacement, connector repair, and PSU-related board damage repair.

Not Sure? Diagnose For Me
